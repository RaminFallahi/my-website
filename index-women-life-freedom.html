WOMEN LIFE FREEDOM.

The WOMEN LIFE FREEDOM website is a online platform built using HTML and CSS that aims to raise awareness about the struggle of Iranian women for their basic rights.

Each title on the pages is a link to a different source website, providing valuable information and resources about the ongoing fight for women's rights in Iran.

The website also features a series of slides, each dedicated to the latest protest incidents in Iran, highlighting the bravery and resilience of Iranian women in their quest for freedom and equality.

Also, the slides have been designed in a loop to convey the message that the fight for women's rights in Iran is ongoing and never-ending.
